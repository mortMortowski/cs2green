How to deposit an item

  1. Select item to deposit
  2. When someone wants to buy your item you'll be notified
  3. You can decide if you want to sell this item to this particular buyer
  4. If accepted, you'll have 5 minutes to send the selected item to the buyer
  5. If everything goes right you'll receive your coins
  6. If there's an error or the time has expired you and the buyer will be locked and you'll have to contact support
  7. Make sure to send the right item! If you send the wrong item you won't be refunded!
